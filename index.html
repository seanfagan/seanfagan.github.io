Projects

Skywind (Mod)

  • Audio Lead
  • Sound Designer
  • Dialogue Editor
  • Implementation

Skywind is a fan-made total conversion mod that recreates TES3: Morrowind in the Skyrim engine. I joined the team in 2020 as sound designer, and quickly got involved in dialogue editing, audio implementation, and eventually became Audio Lead. Now, most of my time is spent creating Trello cards and managing spreadsheets 😉.

  • Visit the official website
  • Watch a gameplay video

Alpha Magus (Game Jam)

  • Sound Designer
  • Composer
  • Implementation

Alpha Magus is a first-person exploration game with light puzzle elements. It was created for the Idle Thumbs game jam. I was the sound designer and composer, and I assisted with the audio implementation in Unity.

  • Get the game on Itch.io
  • Watch a gameplay video

Qubit (Mobile Game)

  • Sound Designer
  • Composer

Control the Garbage Collector as he attempts various puzzles and challenges! Available for free on the Google Play Store. This was a two-person summer project with the goal of shipping our first game. I was the sound designer and composer.

Push and Pull (Student Game)

  • Game Designer

Push and Pull is a sidescrolling platformer where you play a team of magnetic misfits. It was exhibited at SGC 2015. I was a game designer focused on game mechanics, unlockable characters, and UI/UX.

  • Watch a video review

Artwist (Game Jam)

  • Game Designer

Artwist is a comedic local multiplayer game. Two players share one controller, and compete to draw the best picture by connecting the dots. This was a weekend jam from a two-person team.
