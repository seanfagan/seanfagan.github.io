Sean H Fagan

designer, developer

Sean lives in Austin, Texas. He was born there too. Sean graduated from the University of Texas at Dallas with a B.A. in Arts and Technology — along the way he studied music composition at the University of North Texas. He now works for yet another Texas university.

  • Push and Pull (Game)

    Role: Game Mechanics Designer

    Push and Pull is a sidescrolling platformer where you play a team of misfits with magnetic powers. A team of 30 students built the game in Unity in four months. The game is available for free download for Windows and Mac.

    • Watch a video review
    • Visit the site
  • Return to Color (Game)

    Role: UI Designer

    Return to Color is a puzzle platformer where you play as the inhabitants of a computer's graphics card and work to restore color to the system. A team of 14 students built the game in Unity in four months.

    • Watch gameplay
    • Visit the site
  • Solar Rim (Game)

    Role: Composer, Sound Designer

    Solar Rim is a multiplayer first-person shooter where you compete for resources in a randomly generated asteroid field. A team of 8 students built the game in Unity in four months.
